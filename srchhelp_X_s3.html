Search tips and examples

Search Type Description Examples
Phrase Multiple words are searched together as an exact phrase. united states supreme court
Truncation Words may be truncated using an asterisk at the end.
Use a single asterisk * to truncate from 1-5 characters. Use a double asterisk ** for open-ended truncation.		 polic*
environm**
Connectors Use "and" or "or" to specify multiple words in any field. Use "and not" to exclude words. dispute resolution and (united states or canada) and not resident*
Proximity Use "within" and a number to specify proximity of words to one another, in any order. employment within 3 discrimination
Field Search Specify fields to search, using field abbreviation. Fields available for this database are
a: (author)
t: (title)
s: (LC subject) 		a:shakespeare and t:hamlet

education and a:missouri and (s:handicapped or s:disabled)
